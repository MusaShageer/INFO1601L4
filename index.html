What is Power Rangers?

Power Rangers is an American entertainment and merchandising franchise based on the Japanese tokusatsu television series *Super Sentai*. It features teams of costumed superheroes who morph into their Ranger forms to fight evil and protect the Earth. Each series typically features a new team with different powers, Zords (giant robots), and villains, though some storylines and characters have crossed over between series. The franchise is known for its action-packed fight sequences, teamwork themes, and moral lessons. It has spanned decades, becoming a pop culture phenomenon with numerous television series, movies, video games, comics, and merchandise.
